GreenPeaks Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GreenPeaks (“GreenPeaks”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the GreenPeaks mobile application (the “App”).

Quick summary: GreenPeaks uses your photos only to provide app features like plant identification and saving your garden. We do not sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Photos you choose to provide (camera photos or images you select from your library) for plant identification, saving to your gallery, or submissions you make.
  • Account information if you create an account, such as email/username/phone (depending on what you enter) and authentication tokens.
  • App usage and diagnostics (e.g., basic logs, crash info) to keep the app reliable and improve performance.
  • Purchase and subscription status to verify Pro access (via Apple/Google purchase systems and our backend verification endpoints).

How We Use Information

  • Provide the App’s core features (identify plants, show results, save items to MyGarden).
  • Process community or personal submissions you choose to send.
  • Verify subscriptions/entitlements and prevent fraud.
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the App (debugging, reliability, feature improvements).

Sharing of Information

We may share information only in limited cases:

  • Service providers that help us run the App (e.g., hosting, databases), under confidentiality obligations.
  • Store platforms (Apple App Store / Google Play) to process and validate purchases.
  • Legal requirements if needed to comply with law or protect rights and safety.

We do not sell your personal information.

Community Submissions

If you submit an “unknown plant” and choose community/public visibility, we may display the submission’s plant info (and associated photo) to other users once approved by moderation logic. Personal submissions remain visible only to you.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations. You can remove local items (e.g., photos saved locally) using your device controls. Account deletion workflows may be added in a later version.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no system is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to grant photo library and camera permissions.
  • You can choose whether submissions are personal or community/public.
  • You can sign out at any time from the Settings panel.

Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided information, contact us.
